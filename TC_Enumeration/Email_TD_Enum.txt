prefix@domain.extension

Invalid Prefix:
Starts with special characters (_.-) -  .valid@gmail.com
Starts with number/characters, followed by continuous special characters - soor__@gmail.com
Having not allowed special characters like #,@ - soo@@gmail.com

Invalid Domain:

Having invalid special characters - soor@gmail#.com
Having multiple continuous special characters - soor@gmail..com


Invalid extensions

Having single digit extension - soor@gmail.c
Having extension with special characters - soor@gmail.c#


Valid Prefix,domain & extension:
with valid special characters - Soor-1@gmail.com
